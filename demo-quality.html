Quality Policy

Zenith Aviation's Quality Management System is Certified to the following standards:

AS9120 Rev A
ISO 9001:2008
FAA AC-0056A

Zenith Aviation, Inc is committed to providing the aviation industry with products and services that meet or exceed our customer's expectations through continual improvement and compliance to quality standards.

1.

The customer is the driving force behind our business and our reason for being here. Customer Satisfaction will be monitored and recorded regularly, and improved upon at every opportunity.

2.

We will maintain a Quality Management System that meets or exceeds the requirements of AS9120 Rev A, ISO 9001:2008, and FAA AC-00-56A.

3.

The Management Team will periodically review the performance and effectiveness of our Quality Policy, Quality Management System and Quality Objectives.

4.

This Quality Policy will be regularly communicated to our employees, and the Management Team will empower all employees and encourage them to take an active role to improve the Quality Management System.

5.

We will maintain a profitable operation to establish our competitive position in the market and reward our stakeholders.

Zenith Ethics Policy

Zenith will conduct its business fairly, impartially, and in an ethical and proper manner. External Provider shall conduct its business fairly, impartially, and in and ethical and proper manner and in doing so external provider will follow Zenith's published Business Code of Ethics, which can be downloaded HERE, or if available, External Provider's version which is consistent with the terms of Zenith's Business Code of Ethics.

Additionally, our External Providers must adhere to the following:

1.

Have an implemented quality management system

2.

Use only approved or customer designated external providers, this is to include process sources such as special processes

3.

Notify Zenith Aviation and obtain our approval and disposition of any non-conforming processes, products, and/or services.

4.

Prevent the use of counterfeit, unapproved, or suspected unapproved parts

5.

Notify Zenith Aviation of any changes made to processes, products, and services, as well as changes to their external providers or location of manufacture

6.

Flow down to their external providers all customer requirements and any other applicable requirements

7.

Provide test reports, authorized release certificates, or certificates of conformity as applicable

8.

Retain documented information, including disposition requirements, as well as retention periods

9.

Allow Zenith Aviation, our customer, and regulatory authorities right of access to applicable areas of facilities and to applicable documented information, at any level of the supply chain

10.

Ensure all relevant personnel are aware of their contribution to product and service conformity, product safety, and the importance of ethical behavior
